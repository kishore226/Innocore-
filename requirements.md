## Problem Statement
Many people face difficulty accessing essential community services and public resources due to lack of awareness, language barriers, and complex digital systems.

## Proposed Solution
An AI-powered platform that helps users easily discover, understand, and access community services such as education support, healthcare information, government schemes, and public resources.

## Target Users
- Students
- Local communities
- First-time digital users
- Underprivileged or rural users

## Core Features
- AI-based conversational assistant
- Simple and multilingual interface
- Smart recommendations for relevant services
- Easy-to-understand explanations
- Mobile-friendly access

## Expected Impact
- Improved access to public and community services
- Reduced information gap
- Better awareness and inclusion
- Positive social impact through technology
